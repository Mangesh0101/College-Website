Principal
Samarth Polytechnic

Samarth Polytechnic Belhe is one of the best institute in rural area. It is committed for providing quality education throughout three years of diploma engineering. Our institute got the Excellent grade and very good by the Maharashtra State Board of technical Education. Samarth Polytechnics endeavour is uplift professional standards through quality education. Under the umbrella of this institute five engineering courses are running which are Electrical Engineering, Computer engineering, Mechanical engineering, Civil engineering, E&TC engineering. Each course is having its own importance and huge opportunities in government and private sector field.

Many students of our institute got 100/100 marks in critical subjects like M1,M2,M3,TOM,Communication Skill, Basic Science and Applied Science and secured first rank. We are also committed for giving jobs for each student who is completing diploma engineering from our institute.
