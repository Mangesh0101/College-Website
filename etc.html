E & TC ENGINEERING


The Department of E&TC engineering was established in the year 2008 with diploma in electronics and telecommunication with an intake of 60 students. The department has good instruments and equipped with full-fledged laboratories.

The department has faculty members having well experience in their field. The faculty has succeeded in keeping the lovely atmosphere among the students with timely up gradation in their curriculum and innovative teaching technique. Many of the Alumni are placed in reputed branch of industries and others are pursuing higher education and many of them have started their own firms.
