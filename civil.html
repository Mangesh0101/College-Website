CIVIL ENGINEERING



The Department of Civil Engineering, one of the founding Departments of Samarth Polytechnic, Belhe established in 2011, over the years, has grown tremendously, and is now recognized and consistently ranked as one of the best and major engineering departments for civil engineering. The department has very good infrastructure in the form of well-established laboratories such as Surveying Lab, Concrete Technology Lab, Geotechnical Engineering Lab Mechanics of Structures Lab, Model Lab and other laboratories related to various subjects of Civil Engineering.

With its accomplished faculty, the department focuses on development and consultancy across the disciplines of Structural Engineering, and Construction Technology, besides emphasis on high quality teaching and instruction at diploma levels. The Department disseminates the knowledge gained from its high quality research through training programs and interacts with renowned personalities through workshops and conferences.

Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that subjected with the plan, development, and support of the physical and normally structures including public works like streets, bridges, canals, dams, air ports, sewage frameworks, pipelines, underlying parts of structures and railways
