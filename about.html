About us


Samarth Polytechnic Belhe is established under Samarth Rural Educational institute trust in 2008. It is approved by All India Council of Technical Education, New Delhi, recognized by Government of Maharashtra and affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai. Samarth Polytechnic is awarded by MSBTE Mumbai as “Excellent” institute and “very good” institute. Samarth Polytechnic Belhe is winner of National level project competition held at GMRT, Khodad for every year.

Samarth Polytechnic vision is to be reputed rural educational institute by providing superior and trusted professionals of high caliber. Samarth Polytechnic is committed to explore the rural potential by providing high class education hub, creating entrepreneurs, applying professional standards to contribute in growth and make more lives productive.
