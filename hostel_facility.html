HOSTEL FACILITY


The Hostel has been built within the campus. One seperate building is provided for boys and one separate building for girl students. All the hostels encompass beautiful gardens. Each hostel has rector to monitor and to maintain the discipline of hostel. Hostel rooms are airy, clean and well furnished.

Facilities in Hostel

  • R.O. drinking water with cooler.
  • Hot water Facility. Solar system are fixed to provide hot water.
  • First Aid and Medical Dispensary (Doctor is available during 5pm to 7pm daily)
  • Vehicle for medical emergency is available
  • 24 Hr Power supply with generator backup
  • Wi-Fi Facility in hostel premises is available
  • LCD Television with Dish Connection
  • Daily 5 News paper
  • Separate Gym center for Boys and Girls
  • Guest house for parents in hostel campus
  • Xerox, Laundry, Book stall, general stores.
  • For Safety Fire Extinguishers are install in all hostel
  • Security Person available in each hostel
  • CCTV Install in all hostels
  • Seperate Mess and Canteen for each hostel.
  • Reading room are available in each hostel
  • Compliant register is placed, so that students can register their complaints which may solved with four days
